\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements the Asteroids Video Game. 

Implements the update, draw and (run?) methods from \mbox{\hyperlink{class_c_base4618}{CBase4618}} to create Asteroids. It keeps track of all the missiles and asteroids in play and detects collisions between them. It then computes their lives and whether they should be drawn.
